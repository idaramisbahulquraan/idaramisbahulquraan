Year 2025-2026 and onwards
Foreword
Some changes have been made to the curriculum of Idara Misbah-ul-Quran to align it with contemporary requirements. These changes will be effective from March 2026.
There will be two terms in a year. The syllabus and examination for each term will be separate. The syllabus of the first term must be completed within the first term.
Classroom teaching will be fully in accordance with modern teaching methods. Students will be assessed through quizzes, assignments, presentations, and case studies.
The syllabus will be taught according to a daily distribution plan.
Every month, the teacher will submit a report of the taught syllabus on a prescribed form, and an oral examination based on this report will be conducted monthly.
